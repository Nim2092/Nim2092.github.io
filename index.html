Q & A

KNOW US BETTER

Frequently asked questions. You ask - we answer.

Who are EDUZONE's target audience?
EDUZONE has all the courses for everyone, those who are just starting to learn programming, those who already have basic knowledge or those who want to learn to get a job. Please contact EDUZONE for advice on the most suitable course for you.
What do you need to prepare when studying?
You just need to prepare a laptop to practice coding and pen and paper to note down important knowledge.
If I have unexpected work during the study period, can I make up for it?
You can absolutely make up for classes in the same course that are currently open. If there is no class - EDUZONE will arrange a make-up tutor for you at an appropriate time. In case you have to miss the entire course, you can completely retake the course free of charge. At EDUZONE - you only need to pay for one course and can retake it unlimited times.
When students have questions or concerns related to lessons, which channels will students receive support through?
You will receive 24/7 support via online channels such as Facebook... or direct support at the center.
In addition to the existing courses, does the center plan to open any other courses?
In addition to the courses currently being taught, EDUZONE will open other programming courses if students desire and there are enough classes. EDUZONE always listens to students' feedback and wishes to bring the best experiences when studying at EDUZONE.
